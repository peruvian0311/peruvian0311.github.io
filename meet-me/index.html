Who We Are

A dream is defined as a series of thoughts, images, and sensations occurring in a person's mind during sleep. So, it all begun with a dream and the desire of becoming a pathway to bring the human race to a realization that behind every struggle, there is a stronger or equal positive reaction. Headquarters was built upon a foundation that cannot be broken, dissolved, nor demolished through unshakeable roots, which are called family. Family: The brother(Juan Nunez Fernandez), a former Marine that has served in times of war and lived the struggles young men fighting for freedom are experiencing on foreign soil. Sister one(Norma Nunez), a Law seeking degree student who managed to outweigh the struggles with time of education and a business with her perseverance. Sister two(Sara Nunez), A young actress who has lived chasing her dream, disregarding every man made and mind produced obstacle that has come upon herself. It has been a slow process of cultivation, but the wait has been worth every second, every struggle, and every hardship. Finally, after six long years of hard work, sweat, and tears; we are getting a whiff of what is the beginning of our dream shaping into a real thing.
